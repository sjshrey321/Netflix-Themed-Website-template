# Profile Avatars

Each timeline gets one photo. Drop yours in here with these **exact filenames**:

| Filename | Meaning | Recommended content |
|---|---|---|
| `profile-1month.jpg`  | 1 Month milestone  | An early photo of you two together |
| `profile-3month.jpg`  | 3 Month milestone  | A photo around month 3 |
| `profile-6month.jpg`  | 6 Month milestone  | A photo around month 6 |
| `profile-1year.jpg`   | 1 Year milestone   | A recent photo |

## Specs

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Aspect ratio:** Square (1:1) — will be rendered as a rounded square avatar
- **Resolution:** at least 400×400px
- **Tip:** Use photos that visually show the progression — early goofy/candid → recent polished
